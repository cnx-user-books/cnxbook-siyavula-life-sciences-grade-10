Mitosis and the Cell Cycle

The cell cycle is made of stages including G1, S, G2 and Mitosis.

Cell division by mitosis has five stages: Interphase (which in theory is the phase between divisions and not part of mitosis), Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase and Telophase.

Mitosis is important in growth, repair and asexual reproduction.

Reproduction in simple organisms

Simple organisms such as bacteria and protozoa reproduce asexually using mitosis,

Cancer

Cancer is due to cells dividing out of control by mitosis.

There are many types of cancer.

Cancer can be treated by chemotherapy and radiotherapy.
