Unit 1.4 Plant and Animal Tissues

What is a tissue?

Tissue is a level of organization level between cells and a complete organism. A tissue is a group of cells, not necessarily identical, but from the same origin, that together carry out a specific function. These are called tissues because of they are functioning together. Organs are then formed by the functional grouping together of multiple tissues.
